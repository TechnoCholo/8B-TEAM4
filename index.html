What Is Artificial Intelligence:

Artificial intelligence (or AI) is technology that enables computers and machines
to simulate human intelligence and problem-solving capabilities.

How Artificial Intelligence Helps the Society:

It helps improve access to education, healthcare, and clean water, and can
also aid in the fight against climate change, poverty, and hunger.

What We Did For Our Project:

For this project, each member of the team made a brief poster
explaining the information of a book related to artificial intelligence.

How We Did This:

We did this by first checking which artificial intelligence book we would like to read, then getting
some information about the book and of the author, and finally reading the book and summarizing it.
